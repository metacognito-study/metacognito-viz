Salt Invaders

Metallic capsules drop from the top -- and they all look the same. Each wave shows a RULE. Read the label, decide, and shoot only the ones the rule says to destroy. The colour tells you if you were right only AFTER you fire. Speed climbs every wave. Bosses hit at 5, 10, 15.

  • Move: drag, or arrow keys / A D
  • Fire: tap the screen, or Space
  • Combo: chain correct hits for a score multiplier
  • Scanner: grab the glowing blue capsule to reveal answers
