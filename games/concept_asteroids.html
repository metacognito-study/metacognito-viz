Concept Asteroids

Labelled rocks drift through space -- and they all look the same. Each wave shows a RULE. Read the label, steer, and blast only the rocks the rule names. A correct hit splits the rock into fragments; a wrong hit costs a life. Speed climbs every wave. Bosses hit at 5, 10, 15.

  • Steer: left-half joystick, or Left / Right arrows
  • Thrust: push the joystick, or Up arrow / W
  • Fire: tap the right half, or Space
  • Combo: chain correct hits for a score multiplier
