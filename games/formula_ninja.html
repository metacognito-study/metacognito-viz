Formula Ninja

Metallic pods launch up from below -- and they all look the same. Each wave shows a RULE. Read the label, decide, and SWIPE your blade through only the ones the rule says to slice. The colour tells you if you were right only AFTER you cut. Speed climbs every wave. Bosses hit at 5, 10, 15.

  • Slice: drag a finger (or the mouse) across a pod
  • Spare: let the wrong pods fall untouched for a bonus
  • Combo: chain correct slices for a score multiplier
  • Scanner: slice the glowing blue pod to reveal answers
