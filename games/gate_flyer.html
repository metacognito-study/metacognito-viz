Gate Flyer

Your glider flies right on its own. TAP to flap up; gravity pulls it down. Pillars stream in -- each has a stack of gates, and every gate carries a labelled pod. Each wave shows a RULE. Read the pods and fly THROUGH the gate the rule names. The pod colour reveals right only as you pass. Speed climbs every wave. Bosses hit at 5, 10, 15.

  • Flap: tap the screen, or Space / Up
  • Goal: fly through the gate matching the wave rule
  • Avoid: the solid walls, the floor and ceiling
  • Scanner: fly through the glowing blue star to reveal answers
