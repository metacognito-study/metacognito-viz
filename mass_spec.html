Mass Spectrometry - isotopes & the average atomic mass

A mass spectrometer sorts an element's atoms by mass-to-charge ratio (m/z) and reads out how common each isotope is. The periodic-table mass is the weighted average of these isotopes - heavier on the abundant ones. average mass = Σ (isotope mass × fractional abundance)
