Periodic Trends + Coulomb's Law

Why do atoms shrink across a period and grow down a group? One force: F ∝ Zeff / r² — the nucleus pulling on a valence electron.

F = k · (Zeff × e) / r²   model: Zeff/r², k=e=1
Zeff ≈ Z − (shielding): rises strongly across a period, ~constant down a group  |  r = nucleus→valence distance
