Maxwell–Boltzmann speed distribution

The fraction of gas molecules at each speed. Heat the gas and the peak moves right and flattens — but the total area always stays exactly 1 (every molecule has some speed). R = 8.314 J·mol⁻¹·K⁻¹

vp most probable = √(2RT/M) vavg average = √(8RT/πM) vrms root-mean-square = √(3RT/M)
