Heating / Cooling curve — pure water

Add (or remove) heat at a steady rate to 1.0 mol of pure H₂O and watch its temperature. On a slope the phase warms: q = mcΔT. On a plateau the phase changes at constant T: q = nΔH. 1 atm · pure substance
